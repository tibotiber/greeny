About us.

Established since 1984, Greeny Aquaculture is a pioneer in the ornamental aquatic industry. Recognised by industry peers as a leading supplier and distributor of aquatic products, the Department of Fisheries Malaysia has awarded Greeny Aquaculture with the Certificate of Excellence for 8 consecutive years since 2012.


In 2009, Greeny Aquaculture began its expansion into overseas markets. Through expansion of product offerings as well as constant research to upgrade our EXPORT packing techniques, we are currently shipping to more than 20+ countries worldwide on a monthly basis. Providing the lowest landed unit cost and the healthiest arrival conditions have resulted in positive customer feedback. We are happy to say that most new client relationships begin through positive word-of-mouth reviews.


Greeny Aquaculture was awarded a CITES certificate in 2015, and is thus licensed to ship and export endangered arowana species. We currently provide export packaging, shipping and documentation services to customers worldwide who wish to import arowana.


In 2019, we embarked on E-COMMERCE wholesale distribution to retail pet shops across Malaysia by leveraging on refined export packing techniques as well as improved domestic logistic partners and infrastructures. We can proudly say that we are the first (and currently only) local distributor of wholesale live aquatic products via courier services.
