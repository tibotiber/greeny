Contact us

For enquiries related to export shipments, please contact us at export@greenyaquaculture.com.my to get our latest pricelist as well as freight quotation to your nearest airport.


If you are a supplier of aquatic products and would like to enquire about partnership opportunities, please send over a pricelist as well as freight quotation for Singapore / Kuala Lumpur / Johor Senai airport to import@greenyaquaculture.com.my.


For local retail pet shops who wish to begin purchases from us, please log onto our e-commerce website and purchase via our subsidiary company Aquamark. Our dedicated e-commerce team will liaise with you for further enquiries.


If you wish to pursue career or internship opportunities with us, please send your latest resume and CV to officeadmin@greenyaquaculture.com.my.


For all other general matters, you may drop us an email at info@greenyaquaculture.com.my.
