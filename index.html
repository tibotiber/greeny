Services

We know what makes a successful shipment, and our solutions are simple.

Direct From Source

By sourcing directly from local farms, we eliminate unnecessary broker and agent fees and provide fishes at the lowest possible prices. We are also located in Johor, the main production state accounting for more than 75% of Malaysia's tropical fishes.

Freight Options

Strategically located 2.5 hours from Singapore Changi international airport, 3.5 hours from Kuala Lumpur international airport, and only 30 minutes from Johor regional airport, we provide maximum route and cost options for the convenience of our customers.

Produced For You

We do more than export. We have our own bio-secure farm producing more than 40 popular varieties of fishes. Customers can be sure that they will receive the best offers and quality from us. We can also customize our production to your market needs.

About us

As one of the pioneer Ornamental Fish companies from Malaysia, Greeny Aquaculture has been established for 30 years and is well-reputed as a leading breeder of a diverse range of quality ornamental fish. Both Greeny Aquaculture’s production and export departments have been awarded Certificate of Excellence for three consecutive years since 2012, by the Department of Fisheries Malaysia. The company was established in 1984 by the current Managing Director, Mr Raymond Cheah Wan Leong

 

As a pioneer fish producer in Malaysia, Mr Raymond Cheah is constantly on the forefront of the development and progression of Malaysia’s ornamental fish industry.

  • Founder President – Malaysia Aquarium Fish Breeder Association (MAFBA)
  • Steering Committee Member – Asia-Pacific Ornamental Fish Union (AOFU)
  • Board of Advisors - Ornamental Fish International (OFI)
